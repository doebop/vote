Meet the Candidate

About Eric Rayburn

Lifelong resident. Dedicated leader. Your neighbor.

A Proven Leader Committed to Moving Olive Hill Forward

Eric Rayburn has proudly served the people of Olive Hill on City Council for the past eight years. During that time, he has worked to move our city forward—while also seeing firsthand the challenges that still need to be addressed.

Olive Hill is a community built on hardworking families, strong values, and pride in where we live. Eric believes deeply in that foundation—and believes our best days are still ahead.

As mayor, Eric will bring a results-driven, forward-focused approach to City Hall. He understands that Olive Hill cannot afford to fall behind. From restoring strong financial processes to ensuring the city is positioned to secure state and federal funding, Eric is committed to making sure opportunities are no longer missed.

He will lead with accountability—implementing modern systems that ensure projects are tracked, completed, and done right. From infrastructure like water, sewer, and electric systems to everyday work orders, Eric believes in proactive leadership that delivers real results for the community.

But leadership isn't just about systems—it's about showing up. Eric is committed to representing Olive Hill at every level, building relationships, and bringing resources back to our city. He will continue the work he's already started—supporting parks, beautification, and initiatives that make Olive Hill a place people are proud to call home.

Above all, Eric believes in honest, transparent leadership. He's not afraid to ask the tough questions—and he believes the people of Olive Hill deserve clear, straightforward answers.

This campaign isn't about dwelling on the past—it's about building the future.

A stronger, more accountable, and more forward-moving Olive Hill.
