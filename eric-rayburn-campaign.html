About Eric Rayburn

Lifelong resident. Dedicated leader. Your neighbor.

🏛️

A Neighbor Who Understands Olive Hill

Eric Rayburn has called Olive Hill home his entire life. He has seen this town's potential firsthand — the hardworking families, the small businesses, the natural beauty of Carter County — and he believes our best days are still ahead of us.

As mayor, Eric will bring energy, urgency, and common-sense leadership to City Hall. His motto says it all: More Progress, Faster! No more delays. No more excuses. Real results for real people.

Eric is committed to transparent, accountable government that puts residents first — not politics, not special interests. Just Olive Hill.
